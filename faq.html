FAQ'S Page

Home > FAQ'S

Can I customize my football jersey with a player's name and number?

Absolutely! We offer customization options for many jerseys. Simply select the customization option during the ordering process and provide the desired name and number.

How do I care for and wash my football jersey?

To ensure the longevity of your jersey, turn it inside out before washing in cold water. Avoid using bleach and tumble dry on low heat or air dry for best results.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods, including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other secure online payment options. Your payment information is encrypted for a safe and secure transaction.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your package on our website or the courier's site.

What is your return policy?

We want you to be satisfied with your purchase. If there are any issues with your order, please contact our customer service within 30 days of receiving your item. Visit our Returns & Exchanges page for more details on the return process.

How do I choose the right size for my football jersey?

Refer to our size chart for accurate measurements, and consider ordering a size up for a looser fit. If you're unsure, our customer service team is happy to assist you.
